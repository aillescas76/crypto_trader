## Candidate 2 — New Concept: MaGatedRotationVolScaled
- Signal: MaGatedRotation unchanged (20w MA gate + 4w cross-sectional momentum, top-2)
- New mechanism: EWMA volatility-targeted position sizing
  - Compute per-asset realized vol: EWMA(daily log-return², half-life=5d), annualized
  - Position size = min(target_vol / asset_rv * base_capital, max_position_fraction * base_capital)
  - target_vol = 0.02 (2% daily vol target)
  - max_position_fraction = 0.5 (hard cap: no asset > 50% of portfolio)
- Params: vol_lookback_days=20, target_vol=0.02, max_position_fraction=0.5
- Evidence:
  * Vol clustering (GARCH beta 0.91-0.99) confirmed across BTC/ETH/altcoins
  * Zarattini et al. SSRN 5209907: Donchian+vol scaling → 30% CAGR, Sharpe 1.58, -19% MDD
  * ScienceDirect S1544612325011377: vol scaling improves crypto momentum alpha 0.41% → 1.27% monthly
  * arXiv 2602.11708: ATR-scaled approach → Sharpe 2.41, -12.7% MDD
- Fit: purely additive, no change to entry/exit logic; replaces one constant with dynamic sizing
- Overfitting risk: LOW — single intuitive mechanism, not data-fitted threshold
