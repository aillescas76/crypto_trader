This should preserve the +34.99% validation upside while capping MaxDD by preventing fresh capital deployment into declining positions.

## Candidate 2: VolRegimeCadenceAdxGate (VolRegimeCadence)

**Investigation:** VolRegimeCadence (inv-1773443727904-4593)
**Lever:** Replace vol-ratio discriminator with ADX-based trend detection

**Change vs VolRegimeCadence20260314 (Train 280.67%, Val -5.31%):**
- Remove: vol-ratio (short_vol/long_vol < 1.3) discriminator
- Add: portfolio-average 14-period ADX across all 6 assets
- ADX > 20 → trending regime → 1w rebalance (same as before)
- ADX <= 20 → ranging regime → 3w rebalance (CHANGED from 2w to 3w, aligning with SharpeRank3w proven cadence)
- Keep: Sharpe-normalized ranking, 20w SMA gate, hold_count=2

**Rationale:** Vol-ratio failed because crypto has persistently elevated baseline vol, making the ratio insensitive to regime differences. ADX measures directional trend STRENGTH, not vol level — it correctly distinguishes calm-trending (ADX 20+) from calm-ranging (ADX <20). In crypto 2023-2024 bull market, ADX typically >25, correctly triggering 1w. In choppy 2025, ADX likely <20, correctly triggering 3w. Using 3w (not 2w) as the ranging cadence aligns with the best fixed-cadence result.
